<p>PDFs remain the most widely used format for distributing structured reports — financial statements, regulatory filings, research documents, fund fact sheets, and more. Yet despite their structured appearance, PDFs are <em>not</em> machine-readable. Extracting tables reliably is famously error-prone and often requires hours of manual cleanup.</p>
<p>This is especially true in finance and enterprise environments where analysts rely on Excel for modeling and reporting.</p><img height="1" src="https://feeds.dzone.com/link/22488/17241598.gif" width="1" />
