AWS announced that Amazon Q Developer IDE plugins and paid subscriptions will cease support on April 30, 2027, with new signups blocked by May 15, 2026. The successor is Kiro, an agentic IDE, and the article examines the transition and whether migration trade-offs are justified for backend engineers.
