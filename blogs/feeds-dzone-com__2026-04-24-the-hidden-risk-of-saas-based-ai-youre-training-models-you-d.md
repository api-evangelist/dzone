<p>Every time your organization calls a SaaS AI API, you may be strengthening a model your competitor also benefits from. The more you use it, the more you pay to improve infrastructure you do not own — and may never control.</p>
<h2><strong>Architectural Inversion</strong></h2>
<p>SaaS-based AI doesn’t just introduce risk; it fundamentally inverts the traditional enterprise model. Instead of building internal capability, organizations now pay to improve external models. The learning power — the ability to generalize, adapt, and optimize — is centralized away from the data owner. You’re not just losing privacy; you’re losing leverage.</p>
