The article explores the transformative impact of AI on multi-cloud integration , particularly from the perspective of an industry veteran. It discusses the initial skepticism towards AI tools, the shift to decentralized integration methods, and the advantages AI brings to compliance, security, and API management. The author shares personal experiences from projects in various industries, including healthcare, finance, and tech.
