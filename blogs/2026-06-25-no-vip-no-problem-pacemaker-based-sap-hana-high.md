High availability is a non-negotiable requirement for mission-critical SAP HANA deployments. When a primary database node goes down without an automated failover in place, the business impact is immediate. RHEL Pacemaker has long been the standard cluster manager for SAP HANA High Availability(HA) on Linux; it detects failures, fences misbehaving nodes, promotes secondaries, and orchestrates the full recovery sequence without manual intervention.
