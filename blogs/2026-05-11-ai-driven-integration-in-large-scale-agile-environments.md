Abstract This article explores the integration of AI technologies into Agile frameworks, focusing on large-scale applications such as the Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe). Beginning with personal experiences, the article discusses the synergistic potential of combining AI tools like Splunk and MuleSoft with Agile methodologies to enhance project velocity and foresight. It highlights the importance of maintaining human oversight to balance AI insights, mitigating risks through regular feedback loops.
