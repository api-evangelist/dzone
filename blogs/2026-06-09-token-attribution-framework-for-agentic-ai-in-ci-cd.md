The Silent Killer No One Mentions Until the Bill Comes Most papers about "agentic AI in production" stop where the problem starts: price. Interacting with Claude or GPT requires a natural pace setter: you, reading the generated text. Take the same agent out of chat mode and drop it into CI/CD , nightly batch, webhook handling; the pacing goes away, and you're running the thing purely on computer time, at computer prices.
