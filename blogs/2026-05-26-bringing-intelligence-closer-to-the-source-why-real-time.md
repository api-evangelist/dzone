Artificial Intelligence is rapidly becoming a part of everyday devices — smartphones, cars, cameras, and even home appliances. Traditionally, these systems rely on cloud servers to send, process, and analyze data before making decisions, which increases latency and delays responses. However, many applications require instant decision-making, where even a slight delay can be critical.
