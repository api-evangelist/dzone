<p>This article is a short tutorial on <strong>enumeration types</strong>, also called <a href="https://dzone.com/articles/enums-in-python">enumerations</a> or <strong>enums</strong>. They are one of the fundamental constructs of the Java language, alongside classes, interfaces, annotations, and records. They are particularly useful to represent sets of known and unchangeable values, such as the days of the week or the cardinal directions.</p><img height="1" src="https://feeds.dzone.com/link/23564/17300161.gif" width="1" />
