Mainframe modernization is once again at the center of enterprise conversations. Not because something suddenly broke, but because the environment around it has changed. Organizations are being asked to move faster, integrate more easily with newer platforms, and support initiatives like cloud and AI that weren’t part of the equation a decade ago.
