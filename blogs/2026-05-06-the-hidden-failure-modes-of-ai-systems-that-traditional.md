One of the most unsettling characteristics of AI systems is how often they appear perfectly healthy. Infrastructure dashboards report stable CPU utilization, normal latency levels, and acceptable throughput. No alerts are triggered. From an operational standpoint, the system is functioning exactly as designed. Yet the outputs are wrong.
