<p>In the early days of Chaos Monkey, breaking things at random was almost a badge of honor. Kill a service. Drop a node. Add latency. Watch what happens.</p>
<p>That model made sense when most systems were relatively deterministic, and the primary question was simple: <em>Will the application survive if a component disappears?</em></p>
