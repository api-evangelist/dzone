After implementing contract-first integration across three different microservices architectures, I've learned that the biggest bottleneck in distributed systems isn't technical; it's coordination between teams. When Team A waits for Team B to finish their API before starting integration work, you're throwing away weeks of productivity. Contract-first development flips this model.
