<p>Google NotebookLM is a powerful AI tool for interacting with your documents. However, privacy concerns might prevent you from uploading sensitive data to NotebookLM. There is an open source alternative by means of Open Notebook. All data can be kept local, and you are not restricted to Google's Gemini models. Let's check this out!</p>
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p><a href="https://notebooklm.google/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Google NotebookLM</a> lets you upload your documents and get insights about the documents using Google's Gemini models.
