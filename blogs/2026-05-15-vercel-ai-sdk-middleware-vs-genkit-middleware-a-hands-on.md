Two of the most popular Gen AI frameworks in JavaScript/TypeScript, Vercel AI SDK and Genkit , both ship a middleware system to extend their model calls with cross-cutting behavior: logging, caching, RAG, retries, fallbacks, guardrails, tool approval, etc. On the surface they look very similar. In practice, they sit at different abstraction levels and embody different philosophies.
