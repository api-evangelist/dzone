The recent release of A2UI (Agent-to-User Interface) by Google introduces a standardized, open-source protocol for how AI agents render user interfaces. For MLOps, DevOps, and SRE teams, this moves beyond the brittle "text-only" paradigm of traditional ChatOps into a new era of Agentic Interfaces. The following DZone-style article explores how A2UI works and why it is a critical tool for operational workflows.
