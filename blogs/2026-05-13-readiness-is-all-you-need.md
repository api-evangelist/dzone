In 2017, a group of researchers at Google published a paper called "Attention Is All You Need." It introduced the Transformer architecture, kicked off the era of large language models (LLMs) , and changed everything we know about software, work, and productivity. Nine years later, I find the title almost funny. The technology born from "attention" now demands something we have neglected for years: knowing how to read.
