The robotics industry is undergoing a fundamental transformation. For decades, robots have been confined to narrow, pre-programmed tasks in controlled environments — assembly lines, warehouses, and labs where predictability reigns. Vision-language-action (VLA) models represent a critical breakthrough in this evolution by combining visual perception, language understanding, action generation, and the potential for generalization.
